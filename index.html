1. Overview

The Department of Information Technology ensures that public disclosure documents for the MSCSDF program present program specifications, learning outcomes, KPIs, self-study reports, and accreditation status aligned with NCAAA standards. These disclosures promote transparency and academic quality assurance.

2. Mission & Objectives

Mission:

Prepare highly skilled graduates to compete locally and globally in Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics. Focus on advanced knowledge, hands-on training, competitive-learning, research, professional growth, and ethical values to serve locally and globally.

Objective:

  • PE01: Excel as professionals by applying theory and practice, demonstrated through integrating competitions into assessment and research work.
  • PE02: Continuously advance expertise in system security, incident management, and digital forensics through development of project-based assignments that mimic competitive secure environments.
  • PE03: Follow a career path toward leadership and community service through innovation, ethics, and competitive engagement.
